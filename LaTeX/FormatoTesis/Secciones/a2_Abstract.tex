\begin{abstract}%

Write an abstract about your theses, in a clear a simple manner. It is suggested not to exceed 300 words. Generally, an abstract includes:\par%

\begin{itemize}
    \item a short introduction to the research topic,%
    \item an explanation on why this topic is relevant in your field,%
    \item a statement about what the research gap is,%
    \item your research problem or objectives,%
    \item an indication of your research methods and approach,%
    \item your key message to the readers.%
\end{itemize}

Add 3–5 keywords that describe the contents of your work.\par%
\end{abstract}
